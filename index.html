Newton Monthly Meeting

Welcome to Newton Friends Meeting of the Society of Friends. Informally we are referred to as “Quakers”. Our silent meeting for worship is approximately one hour in length. It is held every first and third Sunday of each month at 10:30 AM. Our meeting for worship with the intention to business is held at 12:00 PM the first Sunday of each month (following the rise of worship). The Meeting is actively involved with gardening projects and providing space for a local eco-charter school. We hope that the listed links may provide clarification on Quakerism as well as our Meeting’s activities.
